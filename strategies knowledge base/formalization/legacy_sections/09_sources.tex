\subsection{Transcript coverage notes}

Two transcript files in the repository were empty at extraction time: the files labelled ``Volatility Collection'' and ``Psychology''. Their themes were therefore reconstructed from neighboring transcript material and from the books, rather than from direct transcript content.

\subsection{Reliability notes}

\begin{itemize}
\item The books are cleaner but sometimes generic.
\item The transcripts are richer but highly informal and discretionary.
\item Numerical thresholds from the transcript corpus should be treated as examples, not universal constants.
\item The strongest reusable content is the \emph{logic of interaction} between visible liquidity, executed flow, context, and risk --- not the exact lot sizes mentioned in historical examples.
\end{itemize}
